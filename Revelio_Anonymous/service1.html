Keyword Network

Graphic Representation of Interactions among Your Searches

The network, generated using Google Takeout's Search Data, depicts user-searched terms as nodes.
Node colors represent search term communities identified through a community detection algorithm.
Node size indicates centrality within the network, reflecting significance.

Two main types of edges are distinguished:

  1. solid edges connect search terms with embeddings, created from pre-trained Language Model, exceeding a threshold of similarity,
  2. while dashed edges connect terms with sequential relationships based on search order.
